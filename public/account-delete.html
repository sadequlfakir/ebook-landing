Account Deletion Request

Request permanent deletion of your Ebook account and data

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will permanently delete all your data including:

  • All financial records and transaction history
  • Customer and supplier information
  • Business reports and analytics
  • Account settings and preferences
  • All uploaded documents and attachments
